## Transmission Specifications

- **First Stage Reduction:** 3:1 (30 teeth / 10 teeth)
- **Second Stage Reduction:** 3:1 (30 teeth / 10 teeth)
- **Total Gear Reduction:** 9:1
- **Output Torque:** 2.5 Nm @ 12A
