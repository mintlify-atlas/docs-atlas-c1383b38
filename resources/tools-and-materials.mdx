## Important Notes

<Note>
  When using drill bits to adjust 3D printed parts, always increase hole diameters in small increments (0.1mm steps). Large diameter increases can cause parts to crack.
</Note>

<Warning>
  Always wear appropriate protective equipment (gloves, safety glasses) when using power tools and adhesives.
</Warning>
